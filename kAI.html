uint& AIFlags()

bit field of AI flags:

  • AIF_ATTACKING=1 << 0
  • AIF_WASSOLID=1 << 1
  • 1 << 2–first attack
  • 1 << 3–heard loud noise
  • AIF_BLOWNAWAY=1 << 4
  • 1 << 5–go back to leash
  • 1 << 6–resurrect
  • 1 << 8–regen delay
  • 1 << 9–see target
  • AIF_NOCHASE=1 << 10
  • 1 << 11–regenerate anim
  • AIF_NOTHINK=1 << 12
  • AIF_RUNNING=1 << 13
  • 1 << 14–get attention
  • 1 << 15–regen delay 2?
  • 1 << 16–heard quiet noise
  • 1 << 17–away from leash
  • 1 << 18–teleport away
  • 1 << 19–teleport move slow
  • AIF_TELEPORTING=1 << 20
  • 1 << 22–wait for cycle
  • 1 << 23–damage panic

These can be observed in-game with debugai.
