Scripts kAngle

A kAngle is simply a float that stays within ±π. Values outside the range aren't simply clamped, but rather converted to an equivalent angle within the range.

This saves you from having to worry about things like equivalent angles offset by multiples of 2π, and the difference between two angles will always be the shorter offset. For example, -0.75π - 0.75π will return π, not -1.5π.
